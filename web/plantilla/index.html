La casa de papel

An enigmatic character named the Professor plans something big: carry out the biggest heist in history. For this he recruits a band of eight people who meet a single requirement, none of them has anything to lose. Five months of imprisonment memorizing every step, every detail, every probability ... and finally, eleven days of confinement in the National Mint, surrounded by police forces and with dozens of hostages in their power to find out if their suicide bet will be everything or nothing.
